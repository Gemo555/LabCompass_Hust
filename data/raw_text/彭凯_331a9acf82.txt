Dr. Kai Peng received his BE, MS, and PHD degrees from Huazhong University
of Science and Technology in 1999, 2002, and 2006, respecitvely. He is currently a professor in the
School of Electronic Information and Communications at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
His research interests are in the areas of edge computing, artificial intelligence, cloud computing,
blockchain technology and network security.
