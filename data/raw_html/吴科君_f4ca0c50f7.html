Profile

Dr. Kejun Wu (IEEE Senior Member) obtained his Ph.D. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2022. He is currently a Lecturer at the School of Electronic Information and Communications, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China. Before that, He was a visiting Phd student at School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (Supervised by Prof. Chau Lap-Pui), Singapore and then worked as a Research Fellow researcher there. His research interests include High-dimensional Vision, Generative Artificial Intelligence, and Multimodal Large Language Models, etc. He has published over 40 papers at top venues including IEEE TMM, TCSVT, NeurIPS, ACM MM, etc. He was rewarded the "Chunhui Cup" Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition for Chinese Overseas Students, China International College Students' Innovation Competition, National Postdoctoral Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition and other awards. He received Letter of Appreciation from Oxford University in his lecturing internship course; He is serving as Young Researcher of the Decision-making Consulting Expert Team of the China Association for Science and Technology, Experts of the UHD World AssociationTechnical Planning Expert Group; He serves as Associate Editor in IEEE Open Journal of Signal Processing, Editorial Board Member of Applied Soft Computing, Journal of Real-Time Image Processing, and Lead Guest Editor in Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation; He served as Session / Area Chairs in IJCNN 2025, AIGC 2025, IEEE ICASSP2024, ISCAS2024, MMSP2023 and other international conferences.
